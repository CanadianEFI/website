Membership

Join the CEFI community today

Become a member of CEFI and take part in shaping a growing community focused on advancing ecological forecasting in Canada.

Membership connects you with a vibrant network of peers and provides many benefits, including the opportunity to vote and run in CEFI steering committee elections, collaborate on working group projects and manuscripts, receive discounts on conference registrations, and more. Join today and help us shape the future of ecological forecasting in Canada.

How to become a member

  • Fill the membership form, where you will be asked to provide your name, affiliation, photo, email, social media, mailing address, and a short one-sentence description of your ecological forecasting work interests. Most fields are optional.
  • By becoming a CEFI member, you also become an EFI member. Note that EFI has separate working groups and a Slack group in addition to those hosted by CEFI.
  • There is no fee for registering as a member.
  • Once registered, you will be listed in the members directories for CEFI (coming soon) and EFI.
  • Registered members can select to receive an EFI mug as a gift in the mail.
